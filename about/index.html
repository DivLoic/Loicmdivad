Bio

I’m Loïc DIVAD, a French software engineer working at Spotify and located in Sweden. That’s when I got involved with event streaming and started making several community contributions. Training, blogging, and speaking at conferences were part of my job as well, back then. Maybe this blog will help me to keep this alive! Hope you enjoy.
